Welcome to Germany

Moving to a new country to study is exciting yet challenging. Globsist ensures your transition to Germany is smooth by offering dedicated support from pre-arrival guidance to complete settlement assistance. We help you focus on your academic, personal, and professional journey.

Our Core Services

Pre-Arrival Counselling

Guidance on essential documents, what to pack, and budget-friendly flights to save money before you arrive.

Airport Pick-up ✈

Seamless arrival with direct transportation to your accommodation so you feel welcomed from day one.

Accommodation Assistance 🏠

Help with finding suitable housing (student residence, shared apartments, etc.) and understanding rental contracts.

Essential Documentation 📝

Support with Anmeldung, Tax ID, Health Insurance, Bank Account, Radio Tax, Pension Number, and SIM card setup.

Understanding Lifestyle 🛒

Guidance on German culture, supermarkets, clothing stores, daily apps, and essentials to feel at home on a budget.
